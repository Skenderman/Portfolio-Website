Frequently Asked Questions

Can I repeat each phase for as long as I want?

Yes! The main point is that you can repeat each phase for as long as you want, especialy the last two phases. You can repeat the fisrt phase until you feel comfortable moving to the next phase.

If I feel tired should I still go wrokout?

Unless you are sick or injured in some kind of way, make it a habit to go to the gym, the workout plan gives your the road its up to you to take it!

Do I need to have access to gym equipment for this workout plan?

Yes, the workout plan is solely planned around a gym, there are some exercises you can do at home on the first phase but almost every exercise requires equipment.

How many days a week do I have to workout?

This workout plan is designed for 4 days a week, for the reason most of us are busy doing other things but still want to get in great shape but dont have enough time. You can repeat the leg day once more if you want to go 5 days a week.
